About us

🌍 Sunwoda: Powering the Future with Innovation

Founded in 1997, Sunwoda Electronic Co., Ltd. has grown from a visionary startup into a global powerhouse in lithium-ion battery technology. Listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange (300207) and the Swiss Stock Exchange via GDRs, Sunwoda is now a name synonymous with energy innovation across continents. To be a respected world-class new energy enterprise. We’re accelerating toward a future shaped by globalization, digitization, intelligentization, and green transformation—with nearly three decades of relentless innovation behind us.

Solutions

Sunwoda’s supply chain collaboration integrates advanced solutions across consumer electronics, automotive power, smart hardware, renewable energy, and ecological innovation—driving efficiency, sustainability, and technological progress through unified global partnerships.

📱Consumer Battery

From smartphones to laptops and tablets, Sunwoda’s Consumer Battery technology powers everyday life with precision and reliability. Compact yet powerful, these batteries are optimized for seamless connectivity, extended usage, and sleek design—enabling smarter, more efficient digital experiences across devices.

🔋Energy Storage System

Sunwoda’s Energy Storage System blends solar, wind, and advanced battery technologies to create a resilient and eco-friendly power infrastructure. Designed for scalability and sustainability, it ensures uninterrupted energy supply in both urban and remote environments—empowering industries to transition toward a greener future.

⚡Power Battery

Engineered for high-performance electric vehicles, Sunwoda’s Power Battery solutions deliver robust energy density, thermal stability, and intelligent integration with drivetrain systems. These batteries redefine mobility by offering longer range, faster charging, and enhanced safety—driving the evolution of smart transportation.

Corporate Culture

Over the course of more than 20 years of development, Sunwoda has consistently upheld the spirit of hard work, self-improvement, pioneering, and enterprise under the leadership of its founder, Mr. Wang Mingwang, and chairman, Mr. Wang Wei. The company has demonstrated unwavering determination and unity, ultimately shaping a distinctive corporate culture rooted in the values of customer success, self-reflection, integrity, passionate dedication, and teamwork.

As Sunwoda continues to grow, it has cultivated a people-oriented work environment—providing employees with thoughtful care in their personal lives and robust support in their professional development. The company offers a broad platform for individual growth, enabling employees to thrive alongside the organization and achieve shared success.

Collaborative Project Hub

Sunwoda and SAIC Motor have partnered on the ZS12MCE HEV project, where Sunwoda will supply battery assemblies for SAIC’s hybrid electric vehicle. This collaboration strengthens Sunwoda’s position in the HEV market and supports both companies’ goals for sustainable and intelligent mobility.

Stay updated with the latest news and insights in the world of energy solutions.

⚡ Powering the Future: The Rise of Battery & Energy Storage Solutions
In a world rapidly shifting toward clean energy, battery and energy storage systems (ESS) have emerged as the backbone of sustainable power. From electric vehicles to smart homes and industrial grids, the demand for reliable, scalable, and eco-friendly energy storage is transforming how we live, move, and consume electricity.
🔋 Why Energy Storage Matters
Energy storage isn’t just about keeping the lights on—it’s about stability, efficiency, and independence. With solar and wind energy becoming mainstream, ESS helps balance supply and demand, store excess power, and reduce reliance on fossil fuels. Whether it’s powering a remote village or stabilizing a national grid, batteries are the silent heroes behind the scenes.
🌱 Lithium-ion: The Gold Standard
Among various technologies, lithium-ion batteries lead the charge. They offer:
High energy density for compact storage
Long cycle life for durability
Fast charging capabilities
Low maintenance and minimal degradation
Sunwoda’s advanced lithium-ion solutions are designed for everything from smartphones to electric buses, ensuring performance and safety at every scale.
🏠 From Homes to Mega Projects
Sunwoda’s SunESS modular energy storage systems are redefining residential and commercial energy use. These plug-and-play units allow homeowners to store solar energy, reduce electricity bills, and stay powered during outages. On a larger scale, Sunwoda supports grid-side projects like the Wuyi Energy Storage Station, showcasing how innovation meets infrastructure.
🌍 Sustainability Meets Innovation
At Sunwoda, sustainability isn’t a buzzword—it’s a blueprint. With a focus on green manufacturing, battery recycling, and global expansion (like the new EV battery plant in Thailand), the company is committed to building a cleaner, smarter energy ecosystem.
